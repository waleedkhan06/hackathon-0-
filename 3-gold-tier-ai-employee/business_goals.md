### Communication Goals

#### Email (Gmail)
- Respond to all emails within 24 hours
- Maintain inbox zero daily
- Flag urgent emails for immediate attention

#### WhatsApp
- Respond to client messages within 2 hours during business hours
- Use keywords: urgent, asap, invoice, payment, help

#### LinkedIn
- Post 3 times per week
- Engage with connections daily
- Share valuable business content

#### Twitter
- Post 5 times per week
- Respond to mentions within 4 hours
- Build thought leadership

#### Facebook
- Post 2 times per week
- Respond to comments within 24 hours
- Share business updates

#### Instagram
- Post 3 times per week
- Engage with followers daily
- Share visual content

### Task Management Goals

- Complete all high-priority tasks within 48 hours
- Review pending approvals daily
- Archive completed tasks weekly
- Identify bottlenecks in weekly audit

### Subscription Audit Rules

Flag for review if:
- No usage in 30 days
- Cost increased > 20%
- Duplicate functionality with another tool
- Not aligned with current business goals

### Weekly Audit Focus Areas

1. **Revenue & Income**
   - Track all income sources
   - Identify payment delays
   - Follow up on outstanding invoices

2. **Task Completion**
   - Analyze completed vs pending tasks
   - Identify bottlenecks
   - Measure time to completion

3. **Communication Metrics**
   - Email response times
   - WhatsApp engagement
   - Social media performance

4. **Proactive Suggestions**
   - Cost optimization opportunities
   - Process improvements
   - Automation opportunities
